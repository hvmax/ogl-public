### Panel Discussion with Chinese Chamber of Commerce

1 September 2024
At On Global Leadership, we seek to provide a variety of analyses about leadership in the global economy and, in particular, life in China. Our lens is always a mixture of political economic analysis combined with boots-on-the-ground experience.
In this post, Doug Guthrie participates on a panel interviewed by Lisa Ritchie, Vice President of the Chinese Chamber of Commerce, Arizona, and Vice President of the Asian American Culture and Art Association of Arizona. Also participating in the panel are John Lee, President of the Chinese Chamber of Commerce, Arizona, and Kaiying Li of AZCNEWS. This session addresses the strength of China’s manufacturing.
