### OGL's Guthrie Saw China Changes Coming, Says New York Times

17 June 2021|China, Economic Development
In aNew York Times storypublished Thursday, June 17, 2021, On Global Leadership's Doug Guthrie is profiled and discusses his leadership work at Apple University in China between 2014 and 2019.
As China became more politically exacting under President Xi Jinping, and more demanding of foreign companies, Guthrie warned his superiors that the company had no Plan B, with so much of its supply chain tied up in China.
“I was going around to business leaders, and I’m like: ‘Do you guys understand who Xi Jinping is? Are you listening to what’s going on here?’” Mr. Guthrie said in an interview. “That was my big calling card.”
