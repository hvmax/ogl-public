### Graceful Leadership with John Baldoni

OGL's Doug Guthrie speaks with leadership guru John Baldoni (@jbaldoni52), exploring why Grace—embodied by Connection, Candor, Courage, Compassion, and Cheerleading—is the missing power-up for navigating crisis, elevating teams, and building community. Grace Under Pressure shows leaders how humility, self-care, and forward-leaning resilience turn setbacks into upgrades and keep people human at the center of every decision.Watch the video on our YouTube Channel.
